Site Name: Farm Fresh Naija

This name represents the website's purpose of promoting and connecting local farmers with consumers in Nigeria, emphasizing the freshness and local origin of the farm produce. Domain: farmfreshnaija.com.ng

Site Purpose:

The site provides a platform for consumers to discover and purchase fresh, locally sourced farm produce directly from farmers. It aims to support the local agricultural industry by promoting farm products, providing recipes, and facilitating transactions between farmers and consumers.

Scenarios:

What types of farm produce are available in Nigeria, and how can I purchase them directly from farmers?

How can I find farmers in my local area who sell fresh produce, and what are their contact details?

Where can I find contact information of the farmers in areas far from my home too?
